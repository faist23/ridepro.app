RideWeather Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

RideWeather Pro does not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for app functionality. The app uses third-party services (e.g., Strava, Garmin Connect) to authenticate users and access their ride data only with explicit consent.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication tokens from third-party services (e.g. Strava, Garmin).
  • Non-identifiable analytics (optional, if you add them later).

How We Use Information

Data is used solely to display ride and weather information within the app. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Contact

For questions about privacy, contact support@rideweatherpro.app.
