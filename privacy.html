Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

RideWeather Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS app or visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication tokens from third-party services (e.g., Strava, Garmin) — only after you explicitly authorize access.
  • Basic usage data and anonymous analytics may be collected to improve app performance.
  • No personal identifying information (name, address, or payment data) is collected by RideWeather Pro directly.

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to provide and improve app functionality — such as fetching your rides, syncing weather forecasts, and optimizing route pacing. RideWeather Pro does not sell or share your data with any third party.

Third-Party Services

RideWeather Pro integrates with external platforms you may choose to connect with, including:

  • Strava – for importing ride data and syncing activity information.
  • Garmin Connect – for viewing and uploading activity data (optional).

Each third-party service maintains its own privacy policy, and you should review those when connecting accounts.

Data Storage & Security

Access and refresh tokens are securely stored on your device using Apple’s Keychain system. No ride data or authentication tokens are ever transmitted to our servers.

Your Consent

By using RideWeather Pro, you consent to this privacy policy.
