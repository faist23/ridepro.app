MergePit

Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026

MergePit does not collect any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app contains no third-party SDKs and makes no network requests of its own.

What stays on your device

Your personal best scores, lifetime stats, and your sound and haptics settings are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Game Center

MergePit uses Apple's Game Center for its optional leaderboards and achievements. If you choose to sign in, the scores you submit and the achievements you earn are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive, store, or have access to your Game Center identity or any personal information through it. Game Center is entirely optional — the game is fully playable without signing in.

Children

MergePit is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
