Frequently Asked Questions

How do cards work?

Swipe through cards at your own pace. The app remembers where you left off, so you'll never see the same card twice in a row.

How do I save a card?

Tap the bookmark icon on any card to save it. Saved cards are collected in your Library, which you can open at any time from the main screen.

How do I access my saved cards?

Tap the bookmark icon at the top of the main screen to open your Library. From there you can read, share, or remove any saved card.

How do I use the widget?

Long-press your home screen, tap the + button in the top corner, and search for PocketWisdom. Choose a small or medium widget. The widget refreshes twice a day with a new card, and you can save directly from it by tapping the bookmark.

My widget looks blank or isn't showing cards.

Open the app once after installing — the widget needs the app to run first to set up its card library. If the widget still appears blank after opening the app, try removing and re-adding it from your home screen.

How do I share a card?

Open a saved card from your Library and tap the share icon in the bottom corner. This creates a clean image of the card you can send to anyone.

How often does the widget update?

The widget shows a new card twice a day, around 8 am and 8 pm. Tapping the widget opens the app and brings that card to the front of your deck.
