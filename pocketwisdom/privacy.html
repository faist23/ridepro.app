Privacy Policy for PocketWisdom

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

PocketWisdom was built to provide a quiet moment of reflection. To honor that goal, we respect your privacy completely.

Data Collection

PocketWisdom collects zero personal data. We do not track your usage, we do not require an account, and we do not use analytics or third-party tracking frameworks.

Local Storage

Any data generated by your use of the app—such as which cards you have viewed or saved—is stored entirely locally on your own device. This data never leaves your phone.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer directly.
