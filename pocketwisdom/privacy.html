Privacy Policy

Effective April 7, 2026

PocketWisdom was built to provide a quiet moment of reflection. To honor that goal, we respect your privacy completely.

Data Collection

PocketWisdom collects no personal data of any kind. We do not track your usage, we do not require an account, and we do not use analytics or third-party tracking frameworks of any kind.

Local Storage

Any data generated by your use of the app — such as which cards you have viewed or saved — is stored entirely on your own device. This data never leaves your phone and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third Parties

PocketWisdom contains no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no external services. Nothing is shared with anyone.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out at support@ridepro.app
